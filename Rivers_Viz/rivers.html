River levels
in the south east.

In the south east of the U.K, numerous rivers drainage basins play a vital role in the supply of water for residential and industrial usage.

After limited rainfall during the first half of the summer of 2014, river levels are running low. Click the points on the map to have a look at the current water levels of various rivers in the south east.
