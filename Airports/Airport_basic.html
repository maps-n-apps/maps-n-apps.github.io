London remains the busiest air travel region in Europe.

Over 130 million passengers used London's airports in 2012, miles ahead of Paris, Frankfurt, Amsterdam or Berlin. Click on the yellow circles to see which airport carries the most passengers.

London serves a mixture of leisure and business passengers, where as provincial airports tend to serve many tourism and holiday destinations, thereby catering primarily for leisure passengers.
