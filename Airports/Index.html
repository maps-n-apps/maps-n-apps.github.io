Europe's Top 100 Busiest Airports.
Do they grow or do they shrink ?

The map shows the geographical distribution of the 100 busiest airports in Europe in 2013. Overall, 76% of these airports recorded an increase in traffic compared to the previous year.

Certains airports, particularly in western Europe did record significant drops in passenger figures from 2012 to 2013. The biggest decreases can be seen in Spain.

Click on the circles to find out more facts about those aiports.

Green circle: Increase in traffic vs 2012. The bigger the circle, the bigger the increase.

Red circle: Decrease in traffic vs 2012. The bigger the circle, the bigger the decrease.
